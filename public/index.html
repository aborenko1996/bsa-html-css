PROTO MAN

By Andrey On 30.07.2019

Proto Man, known as Blues (ブルース Burūsu) in Japan, is the first Robot Master created by Dr. Light even before Mega Man himself was created. Considered the "older brother" to Mega Man and Roll, he first appeared in Mega Man 3 training him to see if he could beat Dr. Wily, aiding Mega Man in his fight against evil.
